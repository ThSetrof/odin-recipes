French Onion Soup

Description

French onion soup is a savory dish with caramelized onions in a broth, topped with toasted baguette and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 8 cups of beef or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 salt and pepper
  • Baguette slices
  • Swiss cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Caramelize Onions
    1. In a large pot, met butter over medium heat.
    2. Add thinly slice onions and cook until golden brown and caramelized, stirring occasionally
  2. Add Broth and Seasonings
    1. Pour in the broth, add thyme, bay leaves, salt and pepper.
    2. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld
  3. Prepare Bread and Chesse
    1. While the soup simmers, preheat the oven and toast slices of baguette.
    2. Grate Swiss cheese
  4. Assemble and Broil
    1. Remove bay leaves from the soup
    2. Ladle the soup into oven-safe bowls
    3. Place a slice of tablespoonsted baguette on top pf each bowl and sprinkle
      generously with grated cheese.
    4. Place the bowls under a broiler until the cheese is melted
      and bubbly, creating a golden crust.
  5. Serve
    1. Carefully remove the bowls from the oven (they will be hot).
    2. Allow the soup to cool for a moment before serving

Enjoy your homemade French onion soup!
