Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato past
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sal, divided, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over
    medium heat until well browned
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 Â½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
