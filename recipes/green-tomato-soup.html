Green Tomato Soup

Description

Smooth and beautiful green tomato soup made with coconut milk or cream
and served with croutons and basil. A simple and delicious way of using unripe green tomatoes at the end of the season.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lb green tomatoes unripe, 750 g
  • 1 onion medium
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 ½ cups vegetable stock
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 leaves basil
  • fine sea salt and black pepper
  • a few drops of sesame oil

Steps

  1. Prepare vegetables: Clean, dry, and chop the green tomatoes. Set
    them aside. Chop the onion and set it aside. Chop the garlic
    and set it aside separately.
  2. Saute: Heat oil or butter in a medium soup pot. Cook the onions
    until translucent, 2-3 minutes, strirring frequently.
  3. Add: chopped garlic, curry powder or paste, and sugar. Stir
    continuously for about 1 minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes, stril well and cook for 3-4 minutes, occasionally
    strirring.
  5. Simmer: Add the stock and bring everything to a boil. Cover the
    pot and simmer on medium-low heat for 20-25 minutes or until
    the green tomatoes are soft.
  6. Blend the soup with an immersion blender.
  7. Pour in the coconut milk or heavy cream, stir well, and bring the
    soup to a simmer again. turn off the heat.
  8. Add the finely chopped basil and stir. Adjust the taste with salt, soup to a simmer again. Turn off the heat.
  9. Serve: Garnish with more basil and maybe a few drops of sesame
    oil if using. Serve with croutons.
